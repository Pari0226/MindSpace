About MindSpace

MindSpace is a mood tracking platform designed to help individuals monitor and understand their emotional well-being over time. With a clean and personalized interface, it enables users to record daily moods, track patterns, and gain valuable insights into their mental health.

Our aim is to create a safe digital corner for self-reflection, mental health awareness, and mood prediction using AI-powered tools. Whether you're just journaling or analyzing mood trends, MindSpace grows with you.
